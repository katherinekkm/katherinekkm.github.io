The World

Who dares to say the world is
filled
With putrid smells of hell to
come,
The world shall hate, shall
crush, ---
We live, we die, and all is
done?

The Light of the World shall
never cease
To those with heart and
will;
The Life of Love will give us
peace
At last when all is
still.

Jessica Hoshino
